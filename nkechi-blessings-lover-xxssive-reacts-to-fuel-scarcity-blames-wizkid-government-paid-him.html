Nkechi Blessings' Lover, Xxssive, Reacts to Fuel Scarcity, Blames Wizkid: &quot;Government Paid Him&quot;

Publish date: 2024-08-01
  • Actress Nkechi Blessings' boyfriend, Xxssive, shared his suspicion about President Bola Tinubu recruiting Wizkid to distract Nigerians from the ongoing fuel scarcity
  • The Afrobeat musician began to throw tantrums online on Sunday, April 28, 2024, and that is all Nigerians have since spoken about
  • Xxssive claimed that the fuel scarcity started on the same day as the fight in the music industry between Wizkid and Davido

Nigerian influencer Eseoghene Obire, widely known as Xxssive, who is also in a romantic relationship with actress Nkechi Blessing, thinks the government may have recruited singer Ayo Balogun, aka Wizkid, to distract Nigerians.

Nigerians began to experience fuel scarcity a couple of days ago, with prices rising to as high as N1,000 per liter in some parts of the country. This follows the ongoing feud between some Nigerian Afrobeat musicians.

The viral video captured the public figure, who was recently praised by his lover, stating that most of the hardship faced by Nigerians is a result of high fuel prices.

He additionally stated that if he were told that the government engaged Wizkid to unsettle the internet, he would not doubt it.

In Xxssive's words:

"Is like President Tinubu paid Wizkid to distract Nigerians from fuel Scarcity and other hardship currently going on in Nigeria with his Fued with Davido, Fuel is now scarce and selling for N700 per litre but it’s not trending because of wizkid"

He added that Nigerians would rather engage in trivial issues, such as fanbase wars, than focus on more important things that are directly affecting them.
