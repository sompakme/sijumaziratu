What Did Gojo Say to Geto in Jujutsu Kaisen 0? Unraveling the Mystery

Publish date: 2024-09-23

Jujutsu Kaisen 0, a popular anime, left fans with a burning question: What did Gojo say to Geto in that unforgettable scene? Let’s dive into the mystery, exploring various speculations and the significance of this moment.

The Intriguing Scene

In Jujutsu Kaisen 0, the ending scene featuring Satoru Gojo and Suguru Geto has captured the hearts of fans worldwide. However, what Gojo whispered to Geto remains undisclosed, igniting curiosity and speculation.

Speculation 1: “WE’LL MEET AGAIN RIGHT?”

One theory suggests that Gojo uttered the phrase, “WE’LL MEET AGAIN RIGHT?” This phrase hints at the cyclical nature of Gojo and Geto’s relationship, suggesting that their paths may cross again, even in conflict.

Speculation 2: “You are my best friend. My one and only.”

Another theory proposes that Gojo expressed, “You are my best friend. My one and only.” This emotional statement underscores the depth of their relationship and leaves a lasting impact.

Gege Akutami’s Clue

The Likely Speculation: “My best friend. My one and only.”

While the true words remain a mystery, “My best friend. My one and only.” is considered the most probable statement. This phrase beautifully reflects Gojo’s profound affection for Geto and aligns with the story’s themes of friendship and complexity.
