Terry Funks Estate Goes On Public Auction With Some Off-The-Wall Items

Publish date: 2024-07-16

Terry Funk passed away at 79-years-old, and the world lost a true legend. He left behind a lifetime worth of memories, and he was an unforgettable performer, but he also had a few belongings as well. Now, some of those items are going to the highest bidder.

Assiter Auctioneers has put up Terry Funk’s estate for public auction. Some of those items are quite interesting, including a vintage Jim Beam train decanter, a vintage windmill set, and a Paradise Alley movie bust from Sylvester Stallone.

You may click here to see the items in Terry Funk’s estate. There are a lot of items up for sale that were once owned by the pro wrestling legend. The description for the listing also goes into a bit more explanation about the listing and some of the items that are included in the auction.

Assiter Auctioneers has been commissioned to liquidate the Estate of Terry Funk @ 418 N Shore Dr, Amarillo, TX  including  Furniture, Vintage/Antique Collectibles, Home decor, Household Goods, Jet Ski, and more!

Preview Time 2:00pm-4:00pm Sunday, Sept 24th & Sunday, Oct 1st 

**Pickup times will be ONLY Friday, October 6th 1PM-5PM & Saturday, October 7th 12PM-4PM @ 418 N Shore Dr, Amarillo, TX  

**PICKUP AND VIEWING WILL BE ON SITE AT 418 N Shore Dr, Amarillo, TX**

It does not appear that there are any pieces of pro wrestling memorabilia in this estate auction. These are items that Terry Funk owned, so that should make everything in this auction incredibly special.

Terry Funk is gone, but he will never be forgotten. Our thoughts are still with Funk’s family and friends during this incredibly sad time. RIP.
