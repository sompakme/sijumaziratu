FAQs

Does Nasty Gal offer free shipping?

Nasty Gal offers free express shipping for orders over $70. Alternatively, express shipping for under this amount costs $14.99, while standard shipping costs $8.99.

Is there a sale page at Nasty Gal?

Yes. While Nasty Gal runs sale events during seasonal periods such as Black Friday, Cyber Monday, Christmas, January sales, and Bank Holidays. Customers can shop the sale all year round by visiting the 'Sale' page of the Nasty Gal website.

Does Nasty Gal have an email newsletter?

Yes - customers can sign up to receive the Nasty Gal email newsletter to stay up to date about the latest clothing drops, sales events, exclusive offers, promo codes, and more.

Does Nasty Gal offer student discount?

Absolutely! Nasty Gal offers a 20% discount as well as express shipping on orders over $85. To access this discount you will need to make an account with Student Beans and verify your student ID. Once you are approved, head to the Nasty Gal website and grab some bargains!

Does Nasty Gal have free returns?

Unfortunately, Nasty Gal doesn't offer free returns and the cost of your return will be deducted from your refund. You have up to 28 days from the date of purchase to send your item back for a refund or exchange. All returned items must be in their original condition and packaging.
