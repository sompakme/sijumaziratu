10 Best Bridal &amp; Wedding Shower Venues For Rent in Chicago, IL

Publish date: 2024-08-23
Sumptuous decor, fine china, and classic drinks…Is it any wonder that tea rooms are one of the most popular bridal shower brunch venues in Chicago?
For luxury appeal, it's hard to beat the Pavilion at The Langham Hotel. Located in the heart of downtown, the Pavilion offers a stunning view of the soaring skyscrapers of River North. The Pavilion also offers their signature Langham Afternoon Tea, a blend first debuted in London in 1865. Live music on Saturday and Sunday afternoons during teatime make this one of the classiest tea rooms for bridal showers in Chicago.
Bridal shower brunch restaurants like Wildfire offer tea and snacks in upscale surroundings with New American cuisine for those looking for a more substantial meal. Seven Chicago locations means it's easy to choose one that is accessible to everyone on your guest list. Their parties and special events team are experienced in ensuring bridal showers and other special events go off without a hitch. They even offer cakes and other baked desserts, saving you the trouble of securing a cake.
