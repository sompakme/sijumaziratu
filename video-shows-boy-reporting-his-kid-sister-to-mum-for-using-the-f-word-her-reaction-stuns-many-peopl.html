The girl reportedly said sh*t and her brother made it a point to report the misdemeanour to their mum. The boy reported it wrongly as he told the mum his sister used an F word instead.

He was caught stammering when he found himself before the mother as he could not say clearly exactly what happened. He kept telling his mum not to be mad at him as it was his sister who said the F word.
