Nickelodeon is honoring Matthew Perry with a new special this weekend that pays tribute to the late Friends actor and movie star. Titled Matthew Perry: Thanks for Being a Friend, the half-hour special will air on Sunday at 10 p.m. PT/ET as part of the channel’s Nick at Nite programming block. Nickelodeon says the tribute will be followed by “fan favorite” episodes of Friends featuring Perry.

You can watch the Matthew Perry tribute on TV through your local cable provider. Don’t have cable? One of the best ways to stream Thanks for Being a Friend and watch Friends online is through DirecTV Stream, which offers Nickelodeon as part of its channel lineup. DirecTV currently offers a five-day free trial that you can use to stream Friends online for free.

How to Watch Friends Online

The best way to watch Friends is through Max (formerly HBO Max), which has all 10 seasons of the hit comedy series available to stream online, along with the 2021 Friends reunion. A subscription to Max starts at $9.99 a month, though Amazon Prime members can get a seven-day free trial to Max right now as part of Amazon’s Prime Video deals.

Note: You’ll have to be an Amazon Prime member to get the weeklong Max trial. Your subscription to Max continues at $15.99/month (for an ad-free plan), or you can cancel before the free trial is up to avoid being charged.

The Max free trial deal on Prime Video is the easiest way to watch Friends online for free, since Max itself does not offer a free trial. Max recently added a title card to the first episode of each season that honors Perry and dedicates the repeat airings to his memory.
