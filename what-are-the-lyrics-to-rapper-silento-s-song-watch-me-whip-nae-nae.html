What are the lyrics to rapper Silento's song 'Watch Me (Whip/Nae Nae)'?

Publish date: 2024-08-03

RAPPER Silento soared to stardom after his "Watch Me" music video was viewed more than a staggering one billion times.

The 23-year-old performer has been accused of fatally shooting his cousin in Georgia.

How many people have viewed 'Watch Me'?

Silento's music video Watch Me (Whip/Nae Nae), which premiered in 2015, has been viewed 1,790,297,735 times as of February 2.

The addictive song also included a dance that would go viral on social media.

 He performed a version for Nickelodeon to promote their Labor Day weekend lineup, according to Deadline.

The song would hit number three on the Billboard Hot 100 chart in 2015.

The rapper was just 16 when he rose to fame.

He describes himself on his Instagram page as an artist, songwriter, ex producer, actor and mentor.

Why was Silento arrested?

The rapper, whose real name is Richard Lamar Hawk, was charged with murderin the January 21 fatal shooting of his cousin Frederick Rooks, 34.

Police found Rooks with several gunshot wounds in Panthersville, Georgia, which is about 10 miles southeast of downtown Atlanta, according to The Atlanta Journal-Constitution.

Surveillance footage captured at least one gunshot and several vehicles driving away from the site.
