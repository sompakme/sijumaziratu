Charlotte Flair Recovering at Home with Andrade After Surgery

Publish date: 2024-09-20

WWE has a few key players who are not able to wrestle right now, but Charlotte Flair is arguably the biggest name who is on their injured list. She was injured during match against Asuka in December, and that is not how she wanted to close out 2023. Now, she is at home and recovering after surgery.

PW Insider reports that Charlotte Flair is now home following knee surgery and initial rehab in Birmingham, Alabama. If all goes well, she could be back by the Fall of 2024.

The Queen has already started posting from home as well. She is with Andrade El Idolo, and trying her best to get around the house. She captioned an Instagram story of the two of them at home with “Date Night.”

Charlotte Flair has been injured before, but this one may be a very long wait to see her wrestle again. She seems to have some great support at home, as Andrade is there.

Of course, an injury like Charlotte Flair’s cannot be rushed, because she has a long road of recovery ahead of her. We will have to see what is next in this story, but Flair is going to make it back to WWE eventually.

On another note, Andrade recently left AEW. His WWE return is now expected, but it’s nice to see he’s available at home for Charlotte Flair in the meantime.
