Is there anywhere to smoke at Denver airport after security?

Publish date: 2024-09-13

Denver International Airport (DEN) has designated smoking areas for travelers who wish to smoke after going through security. As of now, there are three smoking lounges located in Concourse C, near gate C17, and in Concourse B, near gate B80. These smoking lounges provide a designated space for smokers to indulge in their habit without interfering with non-smokers.

These smoking lounges at Denver airport are enclosed areas equipped with ventilation systems to minimize the spread of smoke. They offer a comfortable environment for smokers to relax and have a cigarette before their flight. It’s important to note that smoking is prohibited in all other areas of the airport, including restaurants, restrooms, and gates. So, if you’re looking to smoke after passing through security, these designated smoking areas are your best option.

FAQs about Smoking at Denver Airport
