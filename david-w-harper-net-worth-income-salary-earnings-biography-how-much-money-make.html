David William Harper (October 4, 1961) is an actor from the United States. He started his acting career in The Waltons by starring in The Homecoming with his other TV siblings; he then went on to portray Jim Bob Walton, the youngest of the Walton brothers, on the show. He continued acting after The Waltons ended in 1981, starring in the television miniseries The Blue and the Gray. In 1982, David reprised his role as Jim Bob in the three Waltons films. In 1985, he had an appearance in Fletch, but then seemed to vanish from television. Despite the fact that David has retired from show business, he may still be seen at Waltons events and other collector and memorabilia shows. Fortunately for Waltons fans, David has appeared in each of the subsequent special movie versions of The Waltons, which were released in the 1990s.
