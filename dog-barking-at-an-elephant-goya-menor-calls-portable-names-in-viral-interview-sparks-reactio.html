Dog Barking at an Elephant: Goya Menor Calls Portable Names in Viral Interview, Sparks Reactio

Publish date: 2024-09-02
  • Nigerian hypeman Goya Menor seems yet to have gotten over his beef with controversial singer Portable, and a recent interview confirms it
  • Goya, when he was asked about his beef with Portable, said he sees the singer's attacks as a dog barking at an elephant
  • He said when such happens, the elephant doesn't concern itself with what the dog does but focuses on itself as both can't be compared

Fast-rising Nigerian rapper and hypeman Goya Menor has sparked reactions online with comments about ace street pop artist, Portable describing him with derogatory words.

Goya used derogatory and controversial words to describe Portable during a recent interview with Hip TV when he was asked about his beef with Portable.

Menor responded that the last time he was asked about the beef, he told them he didn't have any issues with Portable. Instead, he thinks the Zazo crooner has issues with himself.
