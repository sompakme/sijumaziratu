Reality Series About Jewish Conversion

Publish date: 2024-08-26

Jenna Jameson has a surprising new gig lined up.

The former porn star is set to star in a new reality series documenting her conversion to Judaism, her fiance, Lior Bitton, told The Times of Israel in an interview.

Bitton, an Israeli diamond dealer, said the reality show is in the early production stages and compared it to Keeping Up With the Kardashians. Bitton did not list any executive producers or production companies attached to the program.

“It’s a bit stressful because I’m not used to being in front of the cameras,” said Bitton. “But I can assure you there won’t be any ‘adult content’ in the show. I’m not going there.”

The couple are set to wed in Israel next year. Jameson was raised Catholic and referred to herself as “very religious” in a 2013 interview with Larry King. However, she announced in June that she was converting to Judaism to marry Bitton.

In recent months, Jameson has made no secret of her path to Judaism, posting photos of Jewish dishes and Hebrew phrases. Last week, she expressed support for Israel with a photo of the flag of Israel painted on her back.

Jameson, whose porn career spanned from 1993 to 2008, most recently appeared on Celebrity Big Brother in the U.K. earlier this year. On the reality show, she kept a separate fridge from the other housemates and ate only kosher foods.

The project reportedly is set to air on Israel’s Channel 2. Jameson also is set to star in the upcoming indie film Limelight.
