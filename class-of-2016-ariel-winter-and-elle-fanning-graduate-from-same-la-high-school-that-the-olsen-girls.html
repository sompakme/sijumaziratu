Class of 2016: Ariel Winter and Elle Fanning graduate from same LA high school that the Olsen girls

Publish date: 2024-09-01

    Actresses Ariel Winter and Elle Fanning bid goodbye to their high school days this week when they graduated from Campbell Hall, Los Angeles. The ceremony took place on Tuesday, and saw both girls sport bright blue gowns and caps for the occasion.

    Elle, 18, was joined on the day by older sister Dakota, 22, who posted a sweet message on Instagram. Alongside a snap of the two of them, Dakota wrote: "Congrats to the grad! #biglittlesister."

    Elle Fanning was supported by sister Dakota

    Ariel, also 18, looked great in her graduation gown, with her vibrant red hair clashing perfectly with the blue. The star recently revealed plans to start at UCLA in the autumn, despite her success.

    Ariel Winter also graduated on the same day

    She said: "You gotta have something else you can do. I've always been interested in law so I think it'll definitely be something that I'll love to do and also go to school for."

    Elle and Ariel join a long list of famous Campbell Hall alumni. Elizabeth, Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen all attended the Los Angeles school, and Dakota is also a fellow grad. The late Simone Battle also attended Campbell Hall. GRL singer Simone, most notable for their hit Ugly Heart, tragically died in September 2014 aged 25.
