High Ponytail Hack for LOTS of Volume | Chris Appleton

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Chris Appleton, expert celeb hairstylist for people like JLo and Kim Kardashian, shares his simple tips for a perfect high ponytail.

This is a look Chris calls the double ponytail, "where effectively you just split the hair into two," he says. "So you just pull [the top half of] your hair back like you would normally and tie it relatively loose, so it doesn't have to be perfect. It can be natural, but this is how I do ponytails for the red carpet."
