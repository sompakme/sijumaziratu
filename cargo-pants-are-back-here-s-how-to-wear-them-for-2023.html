Cargo pants are back, here's how to wear them for 2023

Ganni

Ganni

Credit: Courtesy

Ganni's striped denim take on the classic combat trouser is ideal for weekend dressing. Style with your go-to jumper and trainers, swapping out for a T-shirt and sandals when the temperatures rise.

& Other Stories

& Other Stories

Credit: Courtesy

& Other Stories has put their own spin on the traditional cut of combat trousers and created a straight-legged, fitted version.

Ralph Lauren Collection

Ralph Lauren Collection

Credit: Courtesy

Ralph Lauren Collection's tapered cargo pants are the perfect way to smarten up your wardrobe. Flattering, smart and subtle, the design features a solo pocket with adjustable tabs at the waist and hem.

Re/Done

Re/Done

Credit: Courtesy

A true tribute to the cargo pants of the Noughties, Re/Done trousers are made from 100 per cent cotton, and feature belt loops, a button fly fastening, cargo pockets and cuff zips. This style also comes in plain beige as well as a brown camouflage colourway.

The Frankie Shop

The Frankie Shop

Credit: Courtesy

Freshen up your workwear wardrobe with these stylish, minimalist cargo pants from The Frankie Shop. Wide-legged, the smart trousers feature two cargo pockets and pleating down the legs.

Joseph

Joseph

Credit: Courtesy

Understated and elegant, Joseph's combats are perfectly refined. Cut from wool-blend gabardine with relaxed tapered legs and a belted waist, this is a timeless take on the style.

Arket

Arket

Credit: Courtesy

For a true tribute to classic cargo pants of the Nineties, you can't go wrong with this wide-legged version from Arket. Featuring a drawstring waist, you can wear these high or low depending on your preference.

Reformation

Reformation

Another relaxed style but this time in dark khaki. Reformation's combats are made from deadstock fabric and drape perfectly. These are perfect for throwing on for low-key weekends in the city.

Reiss

Reiss

Credit: Courtesy

Reiss' combat trousers are instantly flattering with a high waist, wide-leg silhouette. Instead of the classic side pockets, these trousers have them on the front of the thighs for a modern take.
