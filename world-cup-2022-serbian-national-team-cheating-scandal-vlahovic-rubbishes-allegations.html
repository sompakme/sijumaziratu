World Cup 2022: Serbian national team cheating scandal: Vlahovic rubbishes allegations

Publish date: 2024-08-01

Dusan Vlahovic of Juventus and Serbia has denied the alleged sex scandal in the Serbian national team with a reported chain of infidelity involving several players with teammates' partners.

In the Serbian press, it was said that Vlahovic had an affair with Ana Cakic, the wife of Predrag Rajkovic, and that is why the stellar striker did not play against Cameroon.

It has also been reported that Nemanja Gudelj, Sevilla midfielder, had a relationship with Sofija Milosevic, partner of Luka Jovic, a former Real Madrid player who now plays for Fiorentina.

Dusan Vlahovic and Ana Cakic deny their alleged affair

Vlahovic had to appear at a press conference to deny the information of his alleged infidelity with Rajkovic's partner.

"I'm sorry to have to start a press conference at the World Cup in this way, but I have to talk about it because there is talk about me," Vlahovic said.

"What we've all read and heard... there is no need to comment on something so absurd. Obviously, these people are bored and have nothing better to do because they are frustrated or angry, but working against the national interest of the team at the moment is obviously their main priority.

"These stories are ridiculous, I just want to protect my name and my integrity, so I will take legal action if necessary."

About his absence in the match against Cameroon the striker explained that "I did not play because I was not fit enough, but now I feel better and I am fully focused on the next match."

Nemanja Gudelj and Luka Jovic laugh at the accusations of cheating

Nemanja Gudelj and Luka Jovic brushed off reports of alleged infidelity by posting a picture of the two together and joking as if they were going to fight.

Ana Cakic, Rajkovic's wife, also denies the alleged infidelity.

Ana Cakic, Rajkovic's wife, took to social media to deny the allegations and explain that she has been taking care of her son in the hospital for the last month.
