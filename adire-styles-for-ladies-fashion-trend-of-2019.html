Skirts and trousers

If you want to attract attention, get a patterned skirt or trousers. A skirt with such an ornament effectively sets you apart from the crowd. But mind: it is better to choose a plain top with as simple as possible tailoring.

After all, you focus on the skirt. The ideal option is a straight midi skirt. In this case, the ornament plays in all its glory.

If you want to sew or purchase a flared skirt or, for example, a skirt with frills, choose the simplest and smallest print. Straight mini-skirts with any ornament and colour are perfect for young girls. But even you, young ladies, should wear a plain top for a skirt with a bright print.

If we are talking about trousers, then it is better to take straight or slightly flared trousers with the pattern. Besides, they must have a high waist.
