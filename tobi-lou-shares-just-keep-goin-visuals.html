tobi lou Shares &quot;Just Keep Goin'&quot; Visuals

Publish date: 2024-08-13

tobi lou is back to bless fans with another visual supplement to one of his many creations. This time around the rapper has dropped off another animated clip for his "Just Keep Goin'" track originally found on his tobi lou and The Juice project of last year.

In the clip, animated by Ronald Grandpey, the soul of our familiar animated version of tobi escapes in the middle of the night to go off and enjoy in his favorite pastimes while tobi's unique delivery bounces over the KTwice f. GoodnightMeesh & Tee. Jo-produced joint: "I just wanna cop some heat/What's that on my feet?/That came out this week/When I walk it squeaks."
