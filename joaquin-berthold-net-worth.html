About

Argentine actor most famous for his television roles as Matias LaFontaine on the Disney Channel original series Violetta and Hercules Gonzalez on Giovani Stelle. In 2007, he won the Roberto Jauregui Prize for Best Actor for his performance in the film Walter.

Before Fame

He was a theatre actor who got his start in a production of La Salamanca in 1994. He later studied acting with Carlos Kaspar and attened the Conservatorio Nazionale di Arte Drammatica.

Trivia

He has directed several plays for the theatre group Los Intimos del Teatro.

Family Life
