YoungBoy Never Broke Again Drops Off &quot;Love Is Poison&quot;

Publish date: 2024-08-29

YoungBoy Never Broke Again, formerly known as NBA Youngboy (though still widely referred to as such), recently announced his upcoming album Until Death Call My Name. Last night, he dropped off a new single from the project called "Love Is Poison," which finds him showcasing a different side of his generally rough-around-the-edges persona. The Cash Money AP laced banger finds NBA in his feelings, lamenting over a relationship gone left; it's a tender bit of thug love, with the usual trappings of a Bonnie-And-Clyde style narrative. Unfortunately, an ominous sense of loss lingers throughout, and YoungBoy gets a chance to show off some storytelling prowess.

"Momma told me that she love to see when we together," raps YoungBoy, "I looked and smiled they ain't tellin' you the fuckin' devil." Overall, it's a powerful piece of writing, and showcases some nuance in the young rapper's pen game. Stay tuned for more news on his upcoming project, set to drop in March.
