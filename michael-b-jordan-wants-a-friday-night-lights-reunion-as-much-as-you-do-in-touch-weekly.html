Michael B. Jordan Wants a Friday Night Lights Reunion As Much As You Do! - In Touch Weekly

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Clear eyes. Full hearts. Can’t lose. Even after all this time!

It’s been more than five years since Friday Night Lights went off the air and we left the town of Dillon behind. But at least one of the show’s stars — Michael B. Jordan, aka “Vince Howard” — is gunning to return to his old stomping grounds!

RELATED: See the Cast of ‘Friday Night Lights’ Today

“It has to be the right way,” the actor, 29, told E! News. “It’ll be cool to get together with the Dillon Panthers and the East Dillon Lions and just kind of see that. That’ll be pretty cool.”

He also gave insight on where he thought his character might be today.

“Vince is probably coaching,” Michael said. “Maybe Vince went pro. Vince was pretty good. Let’s say Vince went pro, got his mom the house, took care of the house, took care of his mom the way he wanted to. Dad kind of came back around, you know what I’m saying? Got his family together…Maybe plays a couple years and just decides not to get hurt and just decides to coach and try to pass on that knowledge to the next generation. That’ll be kind of cool to see.”

Now THAT’S something we gotta see!

Here’s to hoping that when they said Texas Forever — they really meant “forever!”
