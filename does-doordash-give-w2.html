Does DoorDash send me a 1099?

All Dashers who earn $600 or more within a calendar year will receive a 1099-NEC via Doordash's partnership with Stripe. The 1099-NEC (short for “Non-Employee Compensation") is used to report direct payment of $600 or more from a company for your services.

Is DoorDash income reported to IRS?

Yes, DoorDash does report its dashers' earnings to the IRS since it provides its drivers with 1099-NEC forms. Since dashers are treated as business owners and employees, they have taxes payable whether they are full-time dashers or drive for DoorDash on the side.
