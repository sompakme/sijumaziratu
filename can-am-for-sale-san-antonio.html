Know Your Can-Am Breeds: From Mavericks to Defenders

Can-Am offers a whole family of off-road royalty, each with its own strengths:

  • Can-Am Maverick: Calling all thrill-seekers! The Maverick is a side-by-side UTV built for heart-pounding adventures on challenging terrain.

  • Can-Am Ryker & Spyder: If you crave the open road but with a touch more stability, Can-Am's three-wheeled motorcycles might be your perfect match.

  • Can-Am Defender: This workhorse UTV is built for tough jobs and hauling gear with ease.
