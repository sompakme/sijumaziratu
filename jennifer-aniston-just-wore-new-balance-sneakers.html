Jennifer Aniston Just Wore New Balance Sneakers

Publish date: 2024-08-29

A Jennifer Aniston sighting is rare, but we've been blessed with quite a handful due to the filming of her most recent film Murder Mystery 2 in Hawaii. But the best part of celebrity sightings on location for filming isn't what their costume wardrobe looks like, but what their off-duty looks are when they walk off set. While Aniston's film attire features florals, loose dresses, and sarongs, her off-set looks feature the biggest supermodel sneaker of the moment: New Balance trainers.

Aniston wore the beloved Katie Holmes bucket bag trend that's on the rise with the practical dad sneaker look that's somehow cool again. New Balance sneakers are a quintessential part of the dad-on-vacation uniform, but now they're also what Emily Ratajkowski apparently slips into when heading out to walk her dog. Supermodels and celebrities everywhere are changing the narrative about the former dad shoes and showing that they're as much a part of the off-duty look as a secondhand Galliano Dior bag.

New Balance sneakers have been rising in popularity for some time now, and while some celebrities like Ratajkowski, Katie Holmes, and Jennifer Garner have been wearing them on repeat, Aniston doesn't seem to have worn the brand since 2009, when she wore them to work out. Her endorsement of the brand's popular 327 sneakers with a $2,000 Celine bucket bag feels like further proof that even the most unexpected sneaker skeptics can have a change of heart. Especially when those sneakers are $100 and truly do look good with anything.

As would be expected with a practical and relatively affordable shoe that's known for comfort and is suddenly accepted by the famous fashion elite, New Balance 327 trainers, as well as many of the brand's other styles, are quietly selling out everywhere. Nordstrom has plenty of options, but sizes are selling out left and right. Aniston's sighting in a pair seems to have flown relatively under the radar in terms of celebrity style news, but it's only a matter of time before Aniston fangirls start investing in 327s for themselves. Clearly, everyone else is already doing it.
