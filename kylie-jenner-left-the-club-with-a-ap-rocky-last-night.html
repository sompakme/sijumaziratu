Kylie Jenner Left The Club With A$AP Rocky Last Night

Publish date: 2024-07-10

Kylie Jenner and Tyga are reportedly done, but Jenner seems to be taking things pretty well, as she was right back to the party scene Friday night. It was just another paparazzi-crowded event for Kylie, and while she managed to dodge questions about her recent breakup with T-Raww on the way to her car, the cameras caught an unexpected sight -- ASAP Rocky in the back seat.

Now of course, the fact that the two took a ride home together isn't exactly concrete evidence that Rocky is a rebound, but we can't really rule it out either. Somehow we feel like the Kylie/Tyga drama is far from over, but we'll have to wait and see what happens next.
