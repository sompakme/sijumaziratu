Grandma Threatens To Disown Grandson Over Tattoo On His Arm

Publish date: 2024-08-12
  • A Ghanaian grandmother, reacting to her grandson's tattoo, threatened to disown him in a humorous yet stern manner
  • Benjamin proudly shared an image of his "GOD FIRST" tattoo with his grandmother, seeking her opinion
  • Unamused, the grandmother sent a voice note urging him to remove the tattoo, citing God's disapproval, and warning him of disownment if he refuses

A Ghanaian grandmother has threatened to disown her grandson, Benjamin, for getting a tattoo on his arm.

The young man proudly sent an image of the tattoo, bearing the inscription "GOD FIRST," to his grandmother, seeking her opinion.

However, in a humorous yet stern reaction, the grandmother sent a voice note warning Benjamin to remove it, stating that God detests such acts and that she would disown him if he refused.
