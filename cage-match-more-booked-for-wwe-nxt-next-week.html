Cage Match &amp; More Booked For WWE NXT Next Week

Publish date: 2024-07-23

WWE presented a big show for Heatwave this week, but next week’s episode is also shaping up to be very entertaining. We will get a new #1 contender, and a cage match. So Tuesday night should be busy next week.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE NXT this week.

The Creed Brothers finally revealed themselves as undercover members of the Schism this week on NXT. They kidnapped Ava in the process, and they challenged the Dyad to a cage match for their NXT careers. The Creed Brothers want that Loser Leaves NXT match result reversed.

Also, Tiffany Stratton needs a new challenger, and they will determine that with a match next week. A fatal four-way between Gigi Dolin, Kiana James, Blair Davenport, and Roxanne Perez.

Noam Dar also won the Heritage Cup once again this week. He won’t defend that cup until No Mercy, but an Invitational tournament is starting next week to determine his next challenger.

All that and more is going down on NXT next week, but so far those two matches are enough to pique anyone’s attention. Keep checking back with Ringside News for more on this story, and everything else in the pro wrestling world.
