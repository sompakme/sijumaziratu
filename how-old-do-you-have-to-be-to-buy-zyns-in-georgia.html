So, you're feeling a little stressed, like a chihuahua stuck in a roomba. Maybe you're craving that cool minty... sensation? The Law Lays Down the Law

Here's the not-so-shocking truth: Georgia, like many other states, has a Tobacco 21 Law. This means you gotta be at least 21 years old to purchase any tobacco product, including those nifty little nicotine pouches we all know and... well, maybe not love, but definitely tolerate.

But Wait, There's More! (Except There Isn't)

Now, you might be thinking, "But Sultan, what about a loophole? Can't I just send my older cousin Timmy to buy them for me?" Unfortunately, Timmy (bless his heart) is probably way too busy perfecting his Fortnite dance moves to be your nicotine errand boy. Supplying Zyns to someone under 21 is a big no-no, and comes with a misdemeanor charge. So, don't do it, and don't let Timmy do it either (unless Timmy is secretly a lawyer with a passion for obscure tobacco laws, in which case, go Timmy!).

So You're Stuck? Not Quite!

If you're under 21 and hankering for some nicotine, this might be a good time to explore the wonderful world of sugar-free candy (seriously, some of that stuff is intense). Maybe take up juggling, or interpretive dance. Or, you know, just wait until you're old enough. It'll happen, eventually.
