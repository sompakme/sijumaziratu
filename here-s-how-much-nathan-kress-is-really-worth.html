Here's How Much Nathan Kress Is Really Worth

Publish date: 2024-08-02

After getting his start in show business when he was just a kid, Nathan Kress has acquired a sizable fortune with his years of hard work. He scored his first role in 1998 as the voice of Easy in "Babe: Pig in the City," before wowing the people over at Nickelodeon.

In addition to his role on "iCarly," the actor is remembered for his parts on "Pinky Malinky" and "Alive in Denver," according to IMDb. Plus, the performer has even dabbled in directing, with his director credits from "Henry Danger" and "Game Shakers." The actor also isn't shy about dreaming career-wise, gushing to Collider in 2014, "One of my favorite actors is Denzel Washington so if I was able to do anything with him that would be absolutely amazing."

With his various projects in entertainment, Kress has an estimated net worth of about $1.5 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. Luckily, it looks like the actor has stayed grounded despite his impressive bank account. When Kress isn't on set, he's busy being a dad at home and often shares photos of his backyard-family-fun with his wife and daughters. There's nothing like a grounded actor who's still got it!
