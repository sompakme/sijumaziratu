Publish date: 2024-07-22

The stars brought their fashion A-game for day 14 of the iconic competition...

    Over the course of the 136th Championships, we have seen the likes of Hollywood stars Freida Pinto, Idina Menzel, Brad Pitt, Leslie Mann and Judd Apatow descend to SW19 to take their seats at Centre Court where they watched Sir Andy Murray, Novak Djokovic and Ons Jabeur.
