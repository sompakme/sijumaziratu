&quot;No Lady Will Date You if You Are Broke&quot;: Ghanaian Actress Habiba Sinare Tells Men, Fans React

Publish date: 2024-07-28
  • Ghanaian actress and philanthropist Habiba Sinare has caused a stir online after she advised striving young men to focus on bettering their lives instead of finding love
  • The beautiful actress, in a video, said any man who could not meet his own basic needs does not have to be in a relationship with a woman
  • Many took to her comment section to react to the post as some of them disagreed with her about her utterance
  • Relationship expert, Kingsley Speaks, opined that being broke is not a death sentence

Ghanaian actress Habiba Sinare has advised young striving men to concentrate on bettering their lives rather than worrying their heads over finding love.

She suggested that men who could not afford their own basic needs should not bother being in a relationship.

In a video sighted by Legit.ng the actress also known for her philanthropic works, admonished young men to work hard to become the men they aspire to be, after which they could rightly decide to go for women befitting their status.

"As a broke guy, why are you in a relationship? As a broke guy that cannot afford the basic needs of life, food, shelter, clothing. Why are you in a relationship? Concentrate on yourself, make sure that you become the man you want to be and when you make it, do not, I repeat, do not sit on the same table with a broke girl," she said.

Netizens register varied thoughts

Some netizens who saw the video posted on her TikTok page, @misshabibasinare, thronged the comment section to register thoughts.
